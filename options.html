Location Switcher lets you to switching between sites by click the â˜¯ icon in address bar.

In most cases, you would like to witching between development and production sites.

You may also create a one way route only, allows switch to "http://example.com" from "http://localhost", but can't switch to "http://localhost" from "http://example.com".
It also allows you to create your own loop. For example:

http://dev3.local -> http://localhost/demo (both way)
http://localhost -> http://example.com (both way)
http://dev1.local -> http://localhost (one way)
http://dev2.local -> http://localhost (one way)

or create your custom loop

http://project.local -> http://test1.server (one way)
http://test2.server -> http://test3.server (one way)
http://test3.server -> http://live.server (one way)
http://live.server -> http://project.local (one way)

Please notice that, the preference will not testing any conflicts, and it follows the order to matching the URL.
